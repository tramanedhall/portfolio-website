About Me

Originally born and raised in Winston-Salem, NC. I have spent most of my professional career around water. A swimmer since age 9, and coach since age 15, I have enjoyed many professional and professionional triumphs both in and out of the pool including:

  • In 2017, beacme the first African-American to coach a swimmer to an individual 14 & under state championship in North Carolina Swimming history.
  • Represented the NC Local Swimming Commitee as a member of the multiple select coaching staffs at the state, regional, and national level.
  • Helped countless members of my community learn to swim through a number of initiatives to promote drowning prevention in at-risk communities.

In 2017, I wanted to make a career change and embarked on the rewarding journey to becoming a software engineer after spending nearly a year as a quality assurance analyst for Herbalife International. In 2018, after months of self-teaching, I was accepted into the CS10 cohort of the Computer Science/Full Stack Web Development track at Lambda School. During my time in the program I gained hands-on experience with:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • React
  • Redux
  • Node
  • Express
  • MongoDB
  • Bootstrap
  • Semantic UI
  • Python
  • Django
  • RESTful APIs
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • DevOps
  • Pair Programming
  • Git
  • Authentication with JWT
  • And more...

I am currently learning AngularJS and GraphQL to further add to my skillset. I hope to join an engineering team that will allow my passion for learning new technologies to flourish, and a company culture that serves as an incubator for me to grow my technical skills while solving interesting challenges, and shipping clean, tested, reliable code.
